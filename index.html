edwin randolph oakes -lrb- march 25 , 1818 in pleasant valley , nova scotia -- 1889 -rrb- was a canadian and nova scotian politician and merchant . he was elected to the canadian house of commons as a member of the liberal-conservative party in 1874 to represent the riding of digby . he resigned later that year when he was appointed to the legislative council of nova scotia in october 1874 . oakes was the son of henry oakes and mary randolph , both descended from united empire loyalists from new york state . he was educated in digby county and became a merchant in digby . he married georgina jane bragg . he was named to the legislative council in 1874 . he died in office in 1889 .
